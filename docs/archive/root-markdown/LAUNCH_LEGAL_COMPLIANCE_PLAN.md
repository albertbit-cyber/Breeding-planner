# Launch Legal Compliance Plan

Date: 2026-05-20

## Checklist

- Privacy policy.
- Terms of service.
- Cookie/session notice where required.
- Data deletion/export process.
- User support contact.
- Marketplace listing policy.
- Abuse/report policy.
- Upload/media content policy.

## Data Handling

- Identify personal data stored.
- Identify genetic/lab result data handling expectations.
- Define retention and deletion rules.
- Confirm backup retention.

## Note

This is an engineering checklist, not legal advice. Have the final policy reviewed by qualified counsel before public launch.
